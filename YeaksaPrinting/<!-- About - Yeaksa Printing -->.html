Yeaksa Printing is a Cambodia-based garment and printing company established from a family business in 2010. With over a decade of industry experience, we have grown into a trusted supplier of custom garments, T-shirt printing, and promotional products for businesses, organizations, government institutions, and large-scale events across Cambodia.
